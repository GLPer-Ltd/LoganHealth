# Hero Background Video

Place your hero background video in this folder named `hero-bg.mp4`.

## Recommended specifications:
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (looping)
- **File size**: Under 10MB for fast loading
- **Content**: Wellness, health, nature, or lifestyle themed

## Free video sources:
- [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/videos/) - Free stock videos
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/videos/) - Free videos
- [Coverr](https://coverr.co/) - Free videos for websites

## Tips:
- Choose a video with subtle movement (not too distracting)
- Avoid videos with text or faces
- Ensure the video works well with the semi-transparent overlay
